# GeoJournal Demo Video Script

## Opening hook

“Travel memories are everywhere—camera rolls, map pins, journals and forgotten hard drives.”

“Yet none of them gives us one world we can return to.”

“And after COVID, travel returned with new urgency—people value time, freedom and meaningful experiences more than ever.”

“GeoJournal turns that scattered history into a living, searchable 3D Memory Atlas.”

“It helps you relive where you’ve been—and lets your past guide where you go next.”

## Before recording

- Open `https://geojournal-atlas-srija-a6b0b.web.app/`
- Use the landing page and click **Preview demo**.
- Keep the browser full-screen.
- For a strict 60-second video, say only **Line 1** for each feature, then use the closing line.
- For the complete feature walkthrough, say both lines; it will take about 90 seconds.

## 1. Landing page

**Action:** Click **Preview demo**.

**Line 1:** “Travel memories are scattered across photos, maps and journals.”

**Line 2:** “GeoJournal brings them together in one living Memory Atlas.”

## 2. 3D globe and 2D map

**Action:** Rotate the globe, click **2D map**, then return to **3D globe**.

**Line 1:** “The 3D globe makes my travel history spatial and memorable.”

**Line 2:** “I can switch to a familiar 2D map whenever I want.”

## 3. Mine and Circle

**Action:** Click **Mine**, then **Circle**.

**Line 1:** “Mine shows only my own memories.”

**Line 2:** “Circle adds trusted friends in violet, with clear authorship.”

## 4. Semantic search

**Action:** Search for `great coffee`.

**Line 1:** “Semantic search finds memories by meaning, not just place names.”

**Line 2:** “A search like ‘great coffee’ retrieves the moments that match.”

## 5. Memories

**Action:** Open **Memories**, then show **Journal**, **Insights**, and **Rediscover**.

**Line 1:** “Journal preserves the story, while Insights reveals my travel patterns.”

**Line 2:** “Rediscover resurfaces anniversaries and memories from previous months.”

## 6. Google and Instagram import

**Action:** Click **Sync** and show the accept/reject review screen.

**Line 1:** “Google Timeline and geotagged Instagram exports become suggested pins.”

**Line 2:** “I accept or reject every suggestion before anything is saved.”

## 7. Add Memory

**Action:** Click **Add memory** and show photos, privacy, and co-travelers.

**Line 1:** “A memory connects its place, date, photographs and personal story.”

**Line 2:** “I choose its privacy and tag co-travelers, who accept before sharing.”

## 8. People and shared trips

**Action:** Open **People**, then show **Following**, **Followers**, and **Shared trips**.

**Line 1:** “People brings together followers, following and shared-trip invitations.”

**Line 2:** “I can also open a friend’s public profile and explore their globe.”

## 9. Inspiration and Atlas Market

**Action:** Open **Inspire**, then show **For You**, **Expeditions**, and **Atlas Market**.

**Line 1:** “Inspire combines public travel stories, expeditions and trip groups.”

**Line 2:** “Atlas Market previews creator guides, digital products and travel packages.”

## 10. Profile, themes, Persona, and badges

**Action:** Open **Profile** and show Atlas Persona, themes, and the Trophy Case.

**Line 1:** “Themes and Atlas Persona make every profile feel personal.”

**Line 2:** “Country badges and trophies reward real progress across cities and journeys.”

## 11. Nexus AI

**Action:** Open **Nexus** and click **Find my best trip window**.

**Line 1:** “Nexus means connection—it is GeoJournal’s AI travel copilot.”

**Line 2:** “It connects memories with leave, calendar, month and budget to plan next.”

## 12. GeoJournal Pro

**Action:** Open **Pro** and show the subscription choices.

**Line 1:** “GeoJournal Pro uses a working Dodo Test Mode subscription flow.”

**Line 2:** “Maximem is our roadmap for permissioned, scalable long-term AI memory.”

## Closing line

“GeoJournal helps me remember where I have been—and lets my past guide where I go next.”
